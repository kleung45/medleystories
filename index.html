The "Time Portal" Star Mark

A Portal to
Inner Peace

Medley Stories isn't just an incense brand; it's a narrative vehicle designed to transport users to a state of zen. The visual identity draws inspiration from the cosmic connection between nature and the self.

The "Star/Atom" symbol represents a portal—a microscopic view of an atom or a macroscopic view of a star—bridging the physical act of burning incense with the spiritual journey of meditation. The design language balances playfulness with serenity, using soft textures, natural tones, and spacious typography to evoke a deep sense of calm.

From Concept to Reality

The Creative Journey

Character Evolution

Witnessing the transformation from rough pencil sketches of the meditating duck to the polished, 3D-rendered cosmic frog character. A journey of refining shapes, defining personality, and integrating brand symbols.

Development Process

01

Ideation & Sketching

Exploring the concept of a "Meditating Guide". Initial sketches focused on a duck character using lotus positions and Enso circles to establish a connection with zen practices.

02

Character Definition

The character evolved into a frog—a symbol of transformation and healing. We added cosmic elements like the "Space Crown" and cape to elevate the mascot from ordinary to mystical.

03

Posing & Application

Developing a range of poses to ensure versatility across packaging and digital media. The brand logo was integrated into the character's attire, solidifying the brand identity.

Identity System

The final visual language combines the tactile warmth of the natural world with the precision of cosmic geometry.
