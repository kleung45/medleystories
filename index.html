The Creative Process

From initial meditation concepts to cosmic storytelling. The journey began with simple hand-drawn sketches exploring the themes of zen, time, and inner peace.

Initial Sketches
Concept Phase 01

Meditating Character Study

Early exploration of the "Zen State" using animal avatars. The duck sketches focused on posture, the lotus position, and integrating the 'Om' symbol naturally into the character design.

Character Evolution
Concept Phase 02

The Cosmic Frog

Transitioning to a frog character to symbolize transformation. Adding elements like the "Time Cape" and "Crystal Crown" to introduce the sci-fi/cosmic narrative layer to the brand story.

Pose Exploration
Character System

Versatility & Expression

Developing a range of poses to ensure the mascot works across various touchpoints—from serious meditation to playful interaction. Note the "Star/Atom" logo integration on the t-shirt.

Concept Element
Visual Prop

The Time Drum

A key brand artifact representing the rhythm of meditation and the portal to inner stories.

Final Deliverables

The finalized 3D brand assets ready for packaging and digital campaigns. The mascot "Zen Frog" serves as the brand ambassador, bridging the gap between traditional incense culture and modern digital storytelling.

The Master Meditator

Final high-fidelity render featuring material textures (velvet cape, crystal crown) that convey premium quality.

Product Mascot

Retail Application

The "Daylight" variant of the character, designed for morning blend incense packaging. The yellow skin tone represents energy and awakening.

Brand Message

"Time is not linear.
It is a story we tell ourselves."

Medley Stories invites users to pause time through scent. Each aroma is a key to a different memory or "story portal".
